## Grooming Essentials

Grooming your horse is essential for maintaining their hygiene and building a strong bond. Follow these steps:

1. **Brushing**: Use a curry comb in circular motions to loosen dirt and hair.
2. **Hoof Cleaning**: Use a hoof pick to remove debris and check for signs of thrush.
3. **Bathing**: Bathe your horse occasionally with equine shampoo for a clean coat.

### Benefits of Grooming

- Promotes better circulation.
- Prevents skin infections.
- Builds trust between you and your horse.

## Feeding Guidelines

Proper nutrition is critical to a horse's health. Here's a quick table of feeding essentials:

| **Feed Type**         | **Purpose**                      | **Daily Amount**                |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Forage (Hay/Grass)    | Primary energy source            | 1.5-2% of body weight           |
| Concentrates (Grains) | Energy for high-performance work | Based on workload (consult vet) |
| Supplements           | Vitamins and minerals            | As prescribed by a veterinarian |

> **Tip**: Always provide clean, fresh water to prevent dehydration.
